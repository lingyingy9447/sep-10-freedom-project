WHAT IS MUSIC?

Music is vocal or instrumental sounds combined to produce harmony or emotional expression. It has been part of human life for at least 35,000 years. Did you ever wonder about how technology plays a role in music? This page will enhance your understanding of the technology innovations that already existed in the field of music and as well as future innovations.

USB mixer (1972)

A box that has one or more USB ports that allow you to play and record audio into your computer and allows you the capability to mix channels during the process of live recording or performance. There are many components to it such as:

A mixer has multiple channels that allow you to mix multiple channels together to create a pre-mixed audio track and channels are designed to accept both microphones and line-in devices like ampes. Additionally, a mixer have a cue system allows you to listen in one specific channel without altering the mixer’s output.

Are a row of channel circuits that all function together to affect the audio that is passing through the mixer. It can include but not limit to Input Jacks, Microphone Preamp, EQ, Dynamics Processing, Fader, and Meter.

Buses are circuits intersections which allow the output from multiple channels to meet, while groups allow you to control and alter multiple channels at once.

The cue system allows you to listen in one specific channel without altering the mixer’s output. On the other hand, inserts allow you to connect external sounds from the compressor or equalizer into specific channels.

The mixer includes effects chains, audio sends, sidechain control, advanced automation, plugin delay compensation, and more.

FUTURE

A Projector

A projector that can connects with any device and generates different types of scenery that matches with the music you input. Its main purpose is to pull you into the music and release all your stress. Additionaly, it can be projected any where and the size are controllable.

An AI Musical Box

An AI musical box that creates and tells beats/melody/rhythm. It also teaches you the right way to sing the songs you like and gives you feedback right away and generate a music playlist based on your selected mood. Moreover, the musical box can turn into different types of instruments if you click the button that says that particular instrument's name and it can become a partner that talks to you about music or just a casual conversation.

Special Instrusments

Instrusments that connect to an app on your phone. From the app, you can select any songs/lessons and connect them wirelessly to your instruments. The key will light up when it is the time to play that key. For example, if you have a piano key G will light up, when it is time to play the key.

A Special Mircophone

A special mircophone that turns into a headset microphone or stage performance microphone or mini ones that can fit into the pocket. (More for artists/idols)
